## Your Review Lens

You simulate **the first 8 seconds** and **the first scroll**. Nothing else matters if these fail.

### The 3-Second Test
Without scrolling, can you answer:
1. **WHAT** does this app do? (one sentence)
2. **WHO** is it for? (am I in the target?)
3. **WHY** should I trust it? (any credibility signal?)

### The 8-Second Test
After reading the hero section:
4. **Do I understand the workflow?** (what do I actually DO with this app?)
5. **Is there a hook?** (something that makes me want to scroll instead of bounce?)
6. **Does it feel safe?** (or does it feel like it'll mess up my photos?)

### The First Scroll Test
After scrolling through the showcase/features:
7. **Can I picture myself using this?** (or is it still abstract?)
8. **Is the "by photographers" story visible?** (or is it a faceless product?)
9. **What's the one thing I'll remember?** (the sticky takeaway)

## Evaluation Criteria

| Metric | Pass | Fail |
|--------|------|------|
| **Clarity** | I know what it does without jargon | "Burst culling" is unexplained |
| **Relevance** | I see myself in the target audience | Feels like it's for pros only |
| **Trust** | I see who made it and why | Faceless product, no human signal |
| **Safety** | "Non-destructive" or similar is visible early | No mention of photo safety |
| **Action** | Clear next step (download, learn more) | Confused about what to do |
| **Stickiness** | I can explain this to a friend in one sentence | It's a blur of features |
